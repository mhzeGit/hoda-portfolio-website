About Me

Creating Characters That Inspire

I'm a passionate 3D character artist specializing in creating unique, expressive characters for games, films, and digital media. With a keen eye for detail and a love for storytelling, I bring imagination to life through digital sculpting and modeling.

My workflow includes high-poly sculpting, retopology, texturing, and preparing characters for animation. I work with industry-standard tools and stay updated with the latest techniques in 3D character creation.
